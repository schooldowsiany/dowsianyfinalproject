Chicken & Quinoa Chili

Servings: 7

Protein: ~35g per serving

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs (900g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup quinoa (rinsed)
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) corn, drained
  • 1 can (28 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp cayenne (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: avocado, cilantro, Greek yogurt

Instructions:

  1. Place chicken breasts at the bottom of the crock pot.
  2. Add all remaining ingredients except toppings.
  3. Cook on low for 6–7 hours or high for 3–4 hours.
  4. Remove chicken, shred with forks, and return to the pot.
  5. Stir well and taste to adjust seasonings.
  6. Divide into 7 portions. Serve with optional toppings.

Italian Chicken with Tomatoes & Onions

Servings: 6–7

Protein: ~30–35g per serving

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs (900g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) diced tomatoes (or 4 fresh tomatoes, chopped)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp dried thyme
  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: fresh parsley or basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Lightly season chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet and sear chicken for 2–3 minutes per side (optional for extra flavor).
  3. Place sliced onions at the bottom of the crock pot.
  4. Add seared chicken on top of onions.
  5. Pour diced tomatoes over the chicken.
  6. Add garlic, oregano, basil, thyme, and red pepper flakes. Stir lightly to combine.
  7. Cook on low for 6 hours or high for 3–4 hours until chicken is tender and fully cooked.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley or basil before serving.
  9. Serving suggestion: Serve over zucchini noodles, brown rice, or a side of roasted vegetables for a balanced meal.

Turkey & Vegetable Stew

Servings: 7

Protein: ~30g per serving

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs (900g) lean ground turkey
  • 4 cups chopped carrots
  • 3 cups chopped celery
  • 2 cups chopped onion
  • 3 cups diced sweet potato
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups low-sodium chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: 1 cup green beans or peas

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, lightly brown the ground turkey (optional but enhances flavor).
  2. Place all ingredients into the crock pot.
  3. Cook on low for 6–7 hours or high for 3–4 hours.
  4. Stir well before serving.
  5. Portion into 7 containers for meal prep.
